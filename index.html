A Tyrian-style vertical shooter where every weapon is an audio loop. Fire on the grid, layer the sound, and your run becomes a track.

  • Beat-Synced Weapons

    Each gun fires on a beat division. Stack loadouts to compose your own synthwave arrangement mid-fight.

  • Roguelike Runs

    Procedural sectors, permadeath, meta-progression. Every attempt a new B-side.

  • Co-op (Planned)

    Two-player couch and online. Duets in enemy airspace.

  • Original Soundtrack

    A full analog-flavored score by synth heads who grew up on Tyrian and Raptor.
