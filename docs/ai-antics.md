## Common Tim phrases Claude should recognize

### "About two weeks of work"
Tim's go-to ballpark for estimating a chunk of dev work. Not literal — it's his unit of "a meaningful but not enormous amount." Don't take it as a precise schedule commitment.

### "Let's call it a day"
Session-ending phrase. Means stop here, commit/save state if appropriate, no new work. Not an invitation to squeeze in one more thing.

### "Not even a humble stray bool"
From the "Did you use AI?" dev blog post. Tim's way of saying he doesn't write any code at all anymore, even the smallest piece. Voice tag.
